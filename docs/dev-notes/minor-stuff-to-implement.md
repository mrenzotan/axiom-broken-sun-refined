## Minor Stuff to Implement for Improvement and Polish

### Battle Scene

- [ ] Give player option to cancel Spell casting
  > Currently, once the player clicks on a Spell to cast it, they are committed to casting that Spell and cannot change their mind. It would be a nice quality of life improvement to allow the player to cancel the Spell casting after clicking on it, in case they accidentally clicked on the wrong Spell or changed their mind about which Spell to cast. This could be implemented by allowing the player to click "Esc" key to cancel Spell casting. This option should also be mentioned in the Spell Input Panel, ""Hold [Left Shift] and speak a spell name. Press [Esc] to cancel." This would provide a more user-friendly experience and give players more control over their actions in the battle scene.
